About Me

My name is Alana Keay and I am 25 years old. I joined the navy within a month of graduating high school. I joined the navy and became a helicopter mechanic and was able to travel around the world for a little bit. When my 4 years can to a close I decided that I needed a change and got out just in time to become a stay-at-home mom while going to school at Full Sail online full time. I think I have found a good balance in my life with trying to push myself with learning something I have always had an interest doing. When in high school I was in charge of designing my high school ROTC website. It has always been an interest to me but now I am able to learn to do design and develop for a living. When I was going up everyone said to find something you love to do and you will never have to work a day in your life. I do not know if it is true but I am willing to try.
